Sacrament Taking the sacrament each sunday both renews all other covenants we have made with God. Additionally, there is a unique covenant associated with the taking sacrament. In this covenant we promise to:
  • Always remember Jesus Christ
  • Take the name of Jesus Christ upon us
  • Keep the commandments we have been given by God
In return, God promises that we may always have the Holy Ghost to be with us!
